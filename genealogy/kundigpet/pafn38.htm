1565. Rev. Amos Barr Kendig

Dr. Amos B. Kendig was one of the best known Methodist cleargymen in New England.

Dr. Kendig was born in Lancaster county, Penna., in 1830, removing to Iowa when a young man. Hi at first studied law, but changed to the ministry and was ordained in 1852. In the Civil War he served for a time as chaplain of the 9th Iowa regiment.
