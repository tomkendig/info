Robert S. Stiver, Jr.
Funeral: Graveside at 10 a.m. today at First Free Church of God Cemetery
Gulfport - Robert "Rob" S. Stiver, Jr., 23, died Tuesday, Aug. 12, 1998.
Mr. Stiver was born in Frankfurt, Germany, and had been a resident of Gulfport for 19 years. He attended Bible Baptist Christian School and Mississippi Gulf Coast Community College Perkinston Campus. He had been employed at Windance Country Club for the past three years.
Survivors include his parents, Robert S. Stiver, Sr. and Mary Dobson Stiver of Gulfport; and his grandparents, Doris M. Stiver and Irma Lee Dobson, both of Gulfport.
A graveside service will be at 10 a.m. today at First Free Church of God Cemetery on Three Rivers Road in Gulfport. Riemann Funeral Home, 25th Avenue, Gulfport, is in charge of arrangements.
The family prefers memorials to the American Cancer Society.
