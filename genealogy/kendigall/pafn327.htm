Selected Families and Individuals

Notes

John Stauffer

A miller by trade, he was taxed at Donegal from 1769 to 1772 and then 1774. He was taxed at Mt. Joy in 1773. He was taxed at Paradise Township, York Co., PA from 1779 to 1789. He owned a grist and saw mill and hemp mill at Paradise.
