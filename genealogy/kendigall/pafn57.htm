Selected Families and Individuals

Notes

Ann R. Kendig

Ann attended the 2002 Kendig Gathering with Paul W. Rhoads and toured the Kendig Estate
tour in Willow Street. Ann was quite talkative at the event and David Jay Kendig recorded some
of her stories on video at the time. During the tour Ann did not reviel her age but did recall her
trips as a young and small girl to the Kendig house where she had played in the elevator/dolly
that went between floors of the three story house.
