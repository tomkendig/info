Kendig historic tour and Genealogy share

    Saturday the 8th of July 2006

    Updated 10 January 2006

    There are two events, a tour of Kendig historic sites and a meeting to share and review Kendig Genealogy data and historical items. Talk about the Genealogy - which should put everything fresh in our minds, then join in the Kendig historic tour.

    Kendig Genealogy share

    The 2006 Kendig Genealogy share was not held.


    • Bring a published article about Kendigs that you think other people have not seen
    • Share a family Bible or other Kendig artifact.
    • Bring two birth, death or marriage dates that you question the accuracy of.
    • Present how you record the Kendig data you have presently.
    • Discuss a common computer related problem you have found in dealing with genealogy data
