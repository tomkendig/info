Kendig Gathering (Bi Annual)

The 2008 Kendig gathering will be held on Sunday the 9th of July in the High Pavilion at East Lampeter Community Park just east of Lancaster Pennsylvania, (use this link for a satellite view - note the ball field). Use this link for a review of the 2004 event. This community park is in the middle of Lancaster county just off Witmer road - very close to the Dutch Wonderland Amusement Park. For directions to the park, use this mapquest link or this yahoo driving directions link. The events and venue for the Sunday gathering are described using this link. On the Saturday before the gathering (July the 8th) will be the focus of genealogy and historical work. For tour events, use this link.

Planning continues for a Kendig gathering for the 300th anniversary of Martin Kendig's arrival in North America. This Kendig gathering would be in 2010 in Lancaster Pennsylvania.

In preparation for this gathering, some Kendigs are considering bi annual Kendig gatherings on even years (2000, 2002, 2004, 2006, 2008) also in Lancaster Pennsylvania. If you find it convenient, the odd years work well for reunions among smaller Kendig branches.

The 2004 Kendig gathering was a wonderful event and success in most everyone's eyes. To recall the venue of this event, use this link.

The 2002 Kendig gathering was also a wonderful event and success in most everyone's eyes. To recall the venue of this event, use this link.

The 2000 Kendig gathering was also a wonderful event and success in most everyone's eyes. To recall the venue of this event, use this link.

The first Lancaster County Kendig reunion at Hobson Park was in 1999 and was a pig roast presented by Kenny and Toots Lichty for their friends and family.
