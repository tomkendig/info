Kendig reunion format

    Sunday the 23 of July 2000

    The Kendig reunion will be a covered dish picnic at the East Lampeter park for Kendigs, their families, their relatives and friends. A roast pig should be ready for serving at about 12:30 PM. Drinks, paper needs and plastic ware will be provided under the park pavilion. The park does have a no alcohol provision. Electric outlets will be active for plugging in electric food heaters or appliances. So bring a covered dish, reacquaint with relatives you have not seen in years, see some Kendig's you have never meet before and let the kids play.

    Kendig reunion unstructured activities

    • Eating
    • Talking
    • Sports - Baseball diamond, volleyball, fishing in the creek if you bring your fishing pole.
    • Family and Kid photos


    Kendig reunion event schedule

    9:00 - Park opens.
    9:30 - setup begins
    10:00 - Review of current Kendig Genealogy starts
    11:00 - Presentation of Kendig memorabilia and photos
    12:30 - Most people should be eating!
    2:00 - baseball for those who are into the game
    2:30 - Candy Scramble (for kids)
    3:00 - reunion photo time
    9:00 - park closes

    Kendig reunion location and lodging

    East Lampeter Community Park is located on Hobson Rd just off Witmer Rd near the town of Smoketown east of Lancaster City. Witmer Road runs between Route 30 and Route 340. This location is not far from the Dutch Wonderland Amusement Park. Out of town people are welcome to make arrangements to stay at the Kendig Tourist Home in beautiful downtown Ronks - phone 717.393-5358. Another alternative - more commercial - is the Bird-In-Hand Family Inn who has a web page at "www.bird-in-hand.com".
