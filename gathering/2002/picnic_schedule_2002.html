Kendig gathering format

    Sunday the 4 of August 2002

    Updated 24 May 2002

    The Kendig gathering will be a covered dish picnic at the East Lampeter park for Kendigs, their families, their relatives and friends. Marianne Lichty (Aunt Toots for me) has arranged for a roast pig - should be ready for serving at about 12:30 PM. Ray Kendig will again provide fresh cooked Lancaster county corn. Drinks, paper needs and plastic ware will be provided under the park pavilion. The park does have a no alcohol provision. Electric outlets will be active for plugging in electric food heaters or appliances. So bring a covered dish, reacquaint with relatives you have not seen in years, see some Kendig's you have never meet before and let the kids play.

    Kendig gathering unstructured activities

    • Eating
    • Talking
    • Sports - Baseball diamond, volleyball, fishing in the creek if you bring your fishing pole.
    • Family and Kid photos


    Kendig gathering event schedule

    9:00 - Park opens.
    9:30 - setup begins - registration table opens
    10:00 - Presentation of Kendig memorabilia and photos
    12:30 - Most people should be eating!
    2:00 - baseball for those who are into the game
    2:30 - Candy Scramble (for kids)
    3:00 - reunion photo time
    9:00 - park closes

    Kendig gathering location and lodging

    East Lampeter Community Park is located on Hobson Rd just off Witmer Rd near the town of Smoketown east of Lancaster City. Witmer Road runs between Route 30 and Route 340. This location is not far from the Dutch Wonderland Amusement Park.
