Kendig Gathering (Bi Annual, even years)

The 2010 Kendig gathering will be held on Sunday the 18th of July in the High Pavilion at East Lampeter Community Park just east of Lancaster Pennsylvania, (use this link for a satellite view - note the ball field). Use this link for a review of the 2008 event. This community park is in the middle of Lancaster county just off Witmer road - very close to the Dutch Wonderland Amusement Park. For directions to the park, use this mapquest link or this yahoo driving directions link. The events and venue for the Sunday gathering are described using this link. On the Saturday before the gathering (July the 17th) will be the focus of genealogy and historical work. For schedule information use this link.

Evite is a popular way find out who is coming - and who is not :-(. go to www.evite.com and let the organizers and others know if you are coming! or not coming but still encourage us.

You can also join the Kendig Gathering yahoo group. The group is used for planning the Gathering (most by email).

Planning continues for a Kendig gathering for the 300th anniversary of Martin Kendig's arrival in North America. This Kendig gathering would be in 2010 in Lancaster Pennsylvania.

In preparation for this gathering, some Kendigs are considering bi annual Kendig gatherings on even years (2000, 2002, 2004, 2006, 2008), the second Sunday in July, also in Lancaster Pennsylvania. If you find it convenient, the odd years work well for reunions among smaller Kendig branches.
