Summary of 2004 Kendig tour and gathering

    10th and 11th of August 2004

    There was a sad part of the 2004 reunion. Joann Kendig Myers was missing. Her tragic death in January of 2003 was difficult for many of us. She was missed.

    A small contingent of about 8 people went on the Kendig Tour on Saturday. The Jacob Kendig house in Willow Street, still elequent remains unchanged since Joann Kendig Myers death. The tour continued on to the New Danville Cemetery and Conestoga Cemetery as in previous years.

    Plans remain for a 2006 Kendig gathering. Details for the gathering continue to be found at www.kendig.info. Keep in touch, talk it up and we'll see you there!
