    Kendig Genealogy share

    9:30 AM in Epworth room of the Strasburg Wesley United Methodist Church, 40 W Main St, Strasburg, PA 17579
    directions, clicking here.

    • Share a family Bible or other Kendig artifact.

    • Discuss a common computer related problem you have found in dealing with genealogy data
