Manuscript at the Reformed Church Historical Society, Franklin and Marshall College, Lancaster Pa.

The only stones remaining in the Kendig graveyard, at the old Kendig mill, now Pugh's mill, on the road between Lancaster's Willow Street.

Georg Kindig--d. Nov. 17, 1823 aged 79 years and 9 days.
Elizabeth Kendig, "frau" of Georg Kendig--d. Sept. 6 1828 aged 76 yrs 4 mo. 29 days.
Ann, wife of Abraham Herr--d. Nov. 30, 1838 aged 32 yrs. 9 mo. 24 days
Henry Spahr--b. Jun 11, 1806--d. Mar. 12, 1846

All the other graves, which included those of Henry Gall, and his wife, Elizabeth Kendig, were destroyed by the Conestoga Traction Company, when the Lancaster and Quarryville trolley road was laid. All the stones are said to have been buried somewhere along an old fence, which is no longer standing.
