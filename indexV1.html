Kendig Information On The Web

This web page supports Kendig gatherings, reunions, genealogy and links to other Kendig web pages.

The 2010 Kendig gathering and picnic will be held on Sunday the 18th of July in the High Pavilion at East Lampeter Community Park just east of Lancaster Pennsylvania. Complete details of this event including maps, schedule and venue can be found at www.gathering.kendig.info.

This year marks the 300th anniversary of Martin Kendig's arrival in North America. This Kendig gathering is scheduled the day after the public celebration of this arrival at Lancaster County Roots 300 on the 17 of July at Hans Herr House in Willow Street.

Gatherings are for people that share a common name but might not be related to each other. Reunions normally involve a single common ancestor. This will be a Kendig Gathering for anyone that considers themselves an ancestor of Martin Kendig and a Kendig reunion for descendants of Earl and Mary Kendig of Ronks.

The Kendig Gathering event organization is handled through the Kendig Gathering Yahoo group where you can see photos from previous years and find useful links. You may have to join the yahoo group to send email to the group.

Kendig genealogy is part of the information shared here and at the Kendig gatherings. Many people independently gather genealogy information. This is an attempt to consolidate as much of this Kendig genealogy information as possible. Peter Kundig and his descendants genealogy can be found at www.genealogy.kendig.info.
